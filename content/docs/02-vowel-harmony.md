## What is Vowel Harmony?

Vowel harmony is a fundamental rule in Finnish where vowels within a word must belong to the same group. This affects word endings and suffixes.

## Vowel Groups

Finnish vowels are divided into three groups:

### Back Vowels
- **a, o, u**

### Neutral Vowels
- **e, i**

### Front Vowels
- **√§, √∂, y**

## The Rule

- A word with **back vowels** (a, o, u) takes endings with back vowels
- A word with **front vowels** (√§, √∂, y) takes endings with front vowels
- **Neutral vowels** (e, i) can appear with either group
