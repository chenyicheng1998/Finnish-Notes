## Key Grammar Points

### 1. Verb "olla" (to be) - Present Tense
- min√§ olen (I am)
- sin√§ olet (you are)
- h√§n/se on (he/she/it is)

- te olette (you are, plural)

### 2. Question Formation with "onko"
- Statement: **T√§m√§ on kurssi.** (This is a course.)

- **min√§** (I) ‚Üí **minun** (my) ‚Üí **minulle** (to me)
- **sin√§** (you) ‚Üí **sinun** (your) ‚Üí **sinulle** (to you)
